## Phase 2: Creation & Engagement (The Action Layer)

### 11. Audio Capture Studio (Record Voice)
*   **The Mission:** Turn a simple recording into a sacred act of preservation.
*   **Functional Anchors:** 
    *   The "Vibe" Button: Large, tactile recording trigger.
    *   Sonic Feedback: Real-time waveform or visual frequency representation.
    *   Creative Prompts: Daily linguistic challenges and custom prompt inputs.
    *   Metadata: Language tagging and context setting.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you make an audio recording interface feel as visually exciting as a video filter?

### 12. Video Harvest Screen (Record Video)
*   **The Mission:** A professional-grade portal for uploading cultural video artifacts.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Seamless Upload: File picking or direct capture.
    *   Visual Context: Prompt/Topic overlays.
    *   AI Pre-processing: Auto-thumbnail generation and progress visualization.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Design a video uploader that feels lightweight but suggests "Immersive Quality."

### 13. The Narrator's Space (Tell Story)
*   **The Mission:** A focus-driven environment for long-form oral history.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   The "Breath" Indicator: A timer that feels meditative, not stressful.
    *   Storytelling Tips: Subtle guidance for better narration.
    *   AI Synthesis: A "Generate AI Story" trigger for visual-audio fusion.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you use motion or lighting to help a user feel like they are sitting around a campfire while recording?

### 14. Ephemeral Creator (Create Story)
*   **The Mission:** Rapid-fire 24-hour cultural snapshots.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Multimodal canvas: Image/Video + Text Captioning.
    *   Time-Awareness: Subtle indication of the 24-hour lifespan.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you differentiate a "Cultural Story" from a generic Instagram story?

### 15. The Cinematic Viewer (Story View)
*   **The Mission:** A zero-distraction, full-immersion media player.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Edge-to-Edge Media: Supporting high-quality vertical visuals.
    *   Guardian Info: Subtle overlay of the creator's identity.
    *   Engagement: Quick-react and view tracking.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Create a "minimalist HUD" (heads-up display) that doesn't obstruct the visual art of the story.

### 16. The Whisper Protocol (Voice Call)
*   **The Mission:** Real-time telephony enhanced by invisible AI translation.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   The Pulse: A visual representation of the caller's energy/voice.
    *   Live Captions: A transcript layer that translates in real-time.
    *   Control Suite: Mute, Speaker, and Mismatch toggles.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you visualize two people speaking different languages and "meeting in the middle" through AI?

### 17. The Global Lens (Video Call)
*   **The Mission:** Face-to-face cultural exchange without language barriers.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Dual-Feed Layering: Strategic positioning of local vs. remote video.
    *   Integrated AI Overlay: Real-time translated subtitles that don't cover the face.
    *   Contextual Tools: Switching cameras, adding participants, or in-call chat.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Design a video call interface where the "Translation Subtitles" feel like part of the cinematic experience, not an afterthought.

### 18. The Council Rooms (Groups)
*   **The Mission:** Discovering and managing large-scale cultural communities.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   The Map of Rooms: Categorized "Discover" feed vs. "Member" feed.
    *   Group Identity: Custom badges for language families, regions, or tribes.
    *   Membership Flow: Clear Join/Leave pathways.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you make a group of 10,000 people feel like a small, tight-knit community?

### 19. The Collective Voice (Group Chat)
*   **The Mission:** High-velocity communication for a tribe.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Identity Swiping: Clear distinction between multiple speakers.
    *   Group Voice Logs: Shared audio archives.
    *   Broadcast Typing: Real-time feedback from the community.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you manage the "noise" of a group chat while highlighting the most important cultural contributions?

## Phase 3: Discovery & Utility (The Support Layer)

### 20. The Live Pulse (Live Viewer)
*   **The Mission:** Real-time interaction with shared broadcasts.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Engagement HUD: Floating comments, heart reactions, and translated chat.
    *   Streamer Stats: Live viewer counts and stream health.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Design a chat overlay that feels alive but doesn't distract from the live human performance.

### 21. The Guardian Map (Contact Discovery)
*   **The Mission:** Expanding your personal network of language mentors and peers.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   The "Why Follow" Layer: AI-driven reasons for suggestions (Mutual interest, proximity, expertise).
    *   Influencer Spotlight: High-visibility profiles for Top Guardians.
    *   Contact Sync: "Finding Your People" flow.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you make a "Suggested Users" list look like a VIP guest list?

### 22. The Engine Room (Settings)
*   **The Mission:** Sophisticated control over identity, privacy, and preferences.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Identity & Security: Profile editing, password management.
    *   Linguistic Preferences: Deep control over translation settings.
    *   Referral Center: Managing growth stats and invite payouts.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Make "Administration" feel like "Customization." Even a settings screen should feel premium.

### 23. The Recovery Bridge (ForgotPassword)
*   **The Mission:** A high-trust, low-stress recovery utility.
*   **Functional Anchors:** Clear email identification, verified code input, and success feedback.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you communicate "Total Security" through just white space and typography?

### 24. The Prophet's Log (Invites)
