### 1. The Welcome Experience (Gateway)
*   **The Problem:** Most language apps feel academic or clinical.
*   **The Mission:** Communicate that this is the home of human heritage and futuristic AI storytelling.
*   **Functional Anchors:** 
    *   Visionary Hook: "Preserving global voices through AI."
    *   Feature Quintet: Voice Sharing, AI-Generated Visual Stories, Cultural Archiving, Earnable Rewards, Global Community.
    *   Conversion Path: Enrollment ("Get Started") vs. Recognition ("Sign In").
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you visualize "Voice" and "History" meeting "Future" without using a generic microphone icon?

### 2. The Main Discovery Feed (Pulse)
*   **The Problem:** Traditional social feeds are cluttered and distracting.
*   **The Mission:** Create a sensory-rich environment for consuming audio and video cultural artifacts.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Context Switching: Following (Personal), Discover (Algorithmic), Trending (Viral), Live (Synchronous).
    *   The Interaction Unit: Must handle Audio (Soundwaves/Play), Video (Immersive), and Text (Phrases).
    *   The "Validator" Loop: A native mechanism for the community to verify linguistic accuracy.
    *   The Engagement Suite: High-velocity interactions (Like, Repost, Share).
*   **Expert Challenge:** Redefine the "Card." How does a user interact with a "voice" as naturally as they do a "photo"?

### 3. The Conversation Hub (Connections)
*   **The Problem:** Messaging lists often feel like a "to-do" list of unread pings.
*   **The Mission:** A high-end dashboard for personal and group relationships.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Ephemeral Layer: A "Stories" bar for immediate visual status.
    *   Connection States: Clear hierarchy for 1-on-1 chats vs. Community Groups.
    *   Real-Time Feedback: Presence indicators and active typing states.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you make 100 unread messages feel organized and peaceful rather than stressful?

### 4. The Deep Communication Screen (Dialogue)
*   **The Problem:** Language barriers create friction in real-time chat.
*   **The Mission:** A seamless, multimodal space where AI eliminates the barrier of language.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   The Bilingual Bubble: Supporting the original language and its AI-translated counterpart simultaneously.
    *   Multimodal Input: Fluid switching between dictating (Voice), typing (Text), and capturing (Media).
    *   Instant Escalation: One-tap triggers for Voice or Video calls.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How do you display "Original Text + Translation + Audio Player" in a single message bubble without it looking messy?

### 5. The User Identity (Heritage Profile)
*   **The Problem:** Profiles are usually just a list of static data.
*   **The Mission:** A digital "Legacy Page" showcasing a user‚Äôs contribution to their culture.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Linguistic Statistics: Quantifying the impact of their contributed clips and followers.
    *   Cultural Tags: Explicitly mapping their native tongue and region.
    *   Contribution Gallery: A premium showcase for their Voice/Video/AI-Story artifacts.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Design a profile that feels like a prestigious "Museum Exhibit" of that person's life and language.

### 6. The Asset Archive (Library)
*   **The Problem:** File management is usually boring and purely functional.
*   **The Mission:** A powerful, intuitive workspace for a content creator.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Media Categorization: Clean separation of Audio, Video, and AI Stories.
    *   Status & Metadata: Language labels, duration, and creation dates.
    *   Creation Trigger: A dominant "Compose/Record" entry point.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Make a list of 500 audio files look beautiful and easy to navigate.

### 7. The Onboarding Portal (Sign Up)
*   **The Problem:** Long forms cause user drop-off.
*   **The Mission:** A sophisticated "Guided Journey" into the app‚Äôs ecosystem.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Identity Mapping: Name, Username, Email, Password.
    *   Cultural Grounding: Selecting Primary Language and Geographic Region/Tribe.
    *   Social Entry: Referral/Invite processing.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Turn the "Data Entry" chore into an "Identity Building" experience.

### 8. The Authentication Portal (Sign In)
*   **The Problem:** Sign-in is a barrier to entry.
*   **The Mission:** A frictionless, secure bridge back to the user's world.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Traditional Entry: Email/Password.
    *   Zero-Friction Entry: OAuth (Google).
    *   Recovery: Account reset pathway.
*   **Expert Challenge:** How does a simple login screen communicate "Security" and "Premium Quality" in 2 seconds?

### 9. The Personalization Matrix (Interests)
*   **The Problem:** Checkbox lists feel dated and cumbersome.
*   **The Mission:** A highly tactile and interactive survey of the user's worldview.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Interest Density: Selecting across Languages, Topics (Culture, Music, etc.), and Regions.
    *   Progress Visuals: Showing the user they are almost "Home."
*   **Expert Challenge:** How can you make selecting 10 interests feel as fun as a mobile game?

### 10. The Contribution Economy (Rewards)
*   **The Problem:** Gamification can often feel "cheap" or childish.
*   **The Mission:** A prestigious financial/contribution dashboard.
*   **Functional Anchors:**
    *   Wealth Visualization: Current point balance and value.
    *   The Podium: A high-stakes Leaderboard focusing on the elite contributors.
    *   The Transaction Layer: Sending points to others or redeeming them in the store.
*   **Expert Challenge:** Design a rewards page that looks like a high-end Fintech app for cultural ambassadors.
